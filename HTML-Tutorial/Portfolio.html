  • Smart Hand Guesture Automation For Everyday Utilities

This IoT-based Smart Hand Gesture project enables contactless control of home appliances using gesture recognition. The system integrates LED lights, a smart dustbin, and a fan, all operated through simple hand movements. Using IoT sensors and microcontrollers, gestures are detected and corresponding signals are sent to the devices. A specific gesture can turn LEDs on/off, open or close the dustbin lid, or control the fan speed. The project enhances hygiene, especially in public or kitchen environments, by eliminating physical touch. It demonstrates the power of IoT and automation in creating smart, user-friendly living spaces.
